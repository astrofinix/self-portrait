Aalahanin by JUDE PÉAN

Each portrait revealed is a repository of character sculpted in me. From the contours of my mania, the shadows of my depression, the words I burnt, I yielded, the passions I've ablazed, and the rawness of me. All of which is accessible by how you interact with me, and how I tap in to a place of character - memory - mine and yours. therefore, existing is lovely who takes to time to appreciate its meaninglessness, and indifference.
