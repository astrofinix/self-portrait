Aalahanin by JUDE PÉAN

Aalahanin is to remember, and to remember is to be held in a place of character - in a memory - of mine and yours. Each portrait revealed is a repository of character sculpted in me. A perspective of me, but not the whole of me. From the contours of my mania, the shadows of my depression, the words I yielded, the passions I've blazed, and the rawness of me. All of which is accessible by how you interact with me, and how I tap into you.
